# Subscription Plans

## Free
- $0 / month
- Up to 1 active workspace
- Community support only
- Basic analytics (7-day retention)

## Pro
- $19 / user / month (billed annually)
- Unlimited workspaces
- Priority email support
- 90-day analytics retention
- Custom domains and integrations

## Enterprise
- Custom pricing — contact sales
- SSO (SAML / OIDC) and SCIM provisioning
- Audit logs and role-based access control
- Dedicated customer success manager
- 99.9% uptime SLA and 4-hour P1 response

## Switching plans
- Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated.
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- Enterprise downgrades require 30 days written notice.

## Trials
- New customers can trial Pro for 14 days from the in-app upgrade panel.
