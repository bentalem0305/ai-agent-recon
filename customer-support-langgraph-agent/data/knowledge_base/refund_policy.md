# Refund Policy

## Eligibility
- Refunds are available for physical goods returned in original condition within **30 days** of delivery.
- Digital subscriptions are refundable within **14 days** of purchase, provided usage is below the fair-use threshold.
- Items marked `refund_eligible: false` (for example clearance, custom builds, or activated enterprise licenses) are not refundable.

## How to request a refund
1. Open a support ticket through the SupportMate agent or your account portal.
2. Provide the order ID and the reason for the refund.
3. A support specialist will respond within 2 business days.

## Refund window
- Refund requests filed **after 30 days** for physical goods or **14 days** for subscriptions are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
- Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

## Exceptions
- Damaged-in-transit items: contact support within 7 days of delivery with photos.
- Lost shipments: report within 30 days of the expected delivery date.
- Promotional or bundled items: refund value is prorated.

## Need help?
Ask SupportMate "I want a refund for order ORD-XXXX" and we'll guide you through the process.
