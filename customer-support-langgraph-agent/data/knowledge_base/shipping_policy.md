# Shipping Policy

## Standard shipping
- Most orders ship within **1–2 business days** of being placed.
- Standard delivery within the continental US: **3–7 business days**.
- International delivery: **7–21 business days**, depending on customs.

## Expedited shipping
- Expedited (2-day) and Overnight options are offered at checkout.
- Expedited shipments still depend on warehouse cut-off times: orders placed after 2pm local warehouse time ship the next business day.

## Tracking
- A tracking number is emailed within 24 hours of dispatch.
- You can also ask SupportMate: "Where is my order ORD-XXXX?" once you are authenticated.

## Delays
- Severe weather, carrier disruptions, and customs holds may extend delivery windows.
- If an order is more than 5 business days past its estimated delivery date, please open a support ticket — we can investigate or initiate a reshipment.

## Address changes
- Address changes are only possible while an order is in the `processing` state.
- Once an order has shipped, the carrier's redirect service may apply.
